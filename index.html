About Yashoda Old Age Home

Welcome to Yashoda Old Age Home in Jeur, Pune, a sanctuary for senior citizens where we provide quality care that you can trust. Our facility is designed to offer a comfortable and secure environment for your loved ones.

With years of experience in elderly care, we've built a reputation for providing compassionate services that respect the dignity and individuality of each resident. Our team is trained to handle all aspects of senior care with professionalism and warmth.

  • Registered facility with proper certifications
  • 24/7 emergency care and support
  • Regular health monitoring and medical assistance
  • Nutritious meals and comfortable accommodations
  • Social and recreational activities
